##  Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes retail sales data to help stakeholders understand key performance metrics, identify sales patterns, and make data-driven decisions.  
The dashboard provides insights into sales performance across different regions, product categories, customer segments, and time periods.

---

##  Dataset Description
The dataset used in this project contains detailed information about retail transactions, including:

- **Sales transactions:** Date, product, quantity, sales amount, and discount  
- **Product details:** Category, sub-category, and product name  
- **Customer information:** Customer ID, region, and segment  
- **Store data:** Store ID, location, and sales representative  

##  Business Objectives
The main objectives of this project are:

1. To evaluate sales performance across different dimensions  
2. To identify the top-performing products and regions  
3. To understand customer purchasing behavior and preferences  
4. To monitor revenue growth, profitability, and discount impact  
5. To provide decision-makers with an interactive and visual tool for performance tracking  

---

##  Key Business Questions
1. What are the overall sales trends over time?  
2. Which products and categories contribute the most to revenue?  
3. How do regional sales compare across different periods?  
4. Which customer segments are the most profitable?  
5. How do discounts affect total sales and profit margins?  
6. What are the key drivers of sales growth?  

---

## Tools & Techniques
- **Power BI Desktop:** Used for data modeling, visualization, and dashboard creation  
- **Power Query (M Language):** For data cleaning, transformation, and integration  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** To create calculated columns, measures, and KPIs  
- **Data Modeling:** Relationships built between sales, customer, product, and store tables  
- **Visualization Techniques:** Charts, tables, KPIs, slicers, and drill-through pages for interactive analysis  

---

## Insights
Some of the key insights derived from the dashboard include:

- Seasonal trends show peak sales during holiday months  
- Certain product categories consistently outperform others across regions  
- High discounts do not always correlate with higher profit margins  
- Loyal customers in specific regions drive a significant portion of total sales  
- Store-level performance varies widely, suggesting opportunities for targeted strategies
